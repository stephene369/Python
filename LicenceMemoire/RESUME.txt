Comparative Study of Two Trading Strategies on the BRVM-Agriculture and BRVM-Public Services Indices

In this study, we compare the trading strategy based on moving averages 
to the combined strategy of the stochastic oscillator and the moving 
average convergence divergence on the BRVM-Agriculture and BRVM-Public 
Service indices. The objective of this study was to determine which 
strategy would be the best to use for an investor who wishes to use 
one of these two strategies as a decision-making tool.

Based on the results of our study, the moving average strategy would be 
a judicious choice for an investor looking to enhance their capital 
through the BRVM-Agriculture index, while the combined strategy of the 
stochastic oscillator and the moving average convergence divergence would 
be the best for the BRVM-Public Service index. In order to broaden the 
comparison possibilities to other values, a desktop application has been 
designed, in which all the comparison criteria developed within the framework 
of our study have been implemented. This application provides the possibility 
to quickly compare the moving average strategy and the combined strategy of 
the stochastic oscillator and the moving average convergence divergence on 
any stock values.
